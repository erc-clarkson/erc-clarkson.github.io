Dunkin' Bisquids

An object-orientated PHP project, styled with Bootstrap, that fetches and displays information about a variety of the best biscuits from a MySQL database. Upon selecting a biscuit you can view more in-depth facts about your favourite biscuit with a description, an arbitrary dunking time metric and a link to its wikipedia. Interactive features include being able to upvote and downvote the biscuits within online sessions.
